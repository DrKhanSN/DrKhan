About Me

I am Dr. Shah Nawaz Khan, a dedicated medicinal chemist with a focus on synthetic organic chemistry. I obtained my Master of Philosophy from the University of Malakand and subsequently pursued my Ph.D. at Shanghai Jiao Tong University on a CSC Type A Scholarship. Under the supervision of Professor Zhankui Sun, I developed innovative synthetic methodologies, focusing on the photocatalytic decarboxylative C-O, C-C, and C-N functionalization of carboxylic acids. My work has been pivotal in advancing our understanding of photocatalytic processes, with significant applications in medicinal chemistry.
